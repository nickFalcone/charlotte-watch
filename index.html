Charlotte Watch - Real-time Charlotte NC Dashboard

Live monitoring dashboard for Charlotte-Mecklenburg County providing real-time alerts, weather conditions, flight status, traffic incidents, power outages, and more.

Real-time Monitoring Features

  • Weather Alerts: National Weather Service severe weather warnings and forecasts for Charlotte and Mecklenburg County
  • CLT Airport: Live flight departures and arrivals from Charlotte Douglas International Airport
  • Traffic Incidents: Real-time traffic conditions, accidents, and road closures in the Charlotte metro area
  • Power Outages: Duke Energy outage tracking and restoration estimates
  • CATS Transit: Charlotte Area Transit System service alerts and delays
  • Local Stocks: Real-time quotes for Charlotte-based companies (Bank of America, Lowe's, SPX)

About Charlotte Watch

Charlotte Watch is a comprehensive real-time monitoring tool designed specifically for residents and visitors of Charlotte, North Carolina. Our dashboard aggregates critical information from multiple sources to keep you informed about weather conditions, transportation, infrastructure, and local events affecting the Charlotte-Mecklenburg area.
